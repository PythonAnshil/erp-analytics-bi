# ERP Business Insights Report

## Overview

This report summarizes key business insights derived from the ERP analytics dashboard built using PostgreSQL, SQL analytics queries, and Power BI visualizations.

The dashboard analyzes revenue performance, project profitability, customer distribution, and operational efficiency.

---

# 1 Executive Summary

The business demonstrates strong revenue growth with several highly profitable projects. However, revenue concentration among a small number of customers and uneven employee utilization present potential risks.

---

# 2 Revenue Insights

## Key Observations

• Monthly revenue shows a generally increasing trend.

• A small number of customers contribute a large share of total revenue.

• Payment trends indicate some delayed receivables.

## Implications

The company is financially healthy but could face cash flow pressure if major customers delay payments.

---

# 3 Customer Insights

## Key Observations

• Revenue is concentrated among the top customers.

• Certain industries generate significantly more revenue than others.

## Implications

The business may face risk if a major client leaves.

Customer diversification could reduce this risk.

---

# 4 Project Profitability

## Key Observations

• Some projects generate significantly higher profit margins.

• Certain projects show high budgets but relatively lower profits.

## Implications

Project pricing strategy may need adjustment.

More focus should be placed on high-margin project types.

---

# 5 Operational Insights

## Key Observations

• Employee utilization varies significantly across roles.

• Some employees contribute substantially more work hours.

## Implications

Workload balancing may improve team efficiency.

Resource allocation could be optimized.

---

# 6 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

1. Diversify the customer base to reduce revenue concentration risk.

2. Focus business development on industries with higher revenue potential.

3. Review pricing strategies for lower-margin projects.

4. Improve workforce allocation to balance employee utilization.

5. Strengthen receivables management to reduce overdue payments.

---

# Conclusion

The ERP analytics dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the company's financial performance, project profitability, customer distribution, and operational efficiency. These insights enable management to make informed strategic decisions to improve profitability and operational performance.
